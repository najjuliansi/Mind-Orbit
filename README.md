Mind Orbit is a top-down shooter where you pilot 
a research rocket through 8 planets + a bonus Sun level. 
Each level introduces unique challenges, environments, 
and science facts about the solar system.

Core Gameplay Loop:

Survive enemy & meteor waves
Defeat the planet boss
Unlock the next planet
Collect meteorite pieces for upgrades
Learn real space facts along the way

...
Controls

WASD — Move
Mouse Click/Hold — Shoot

Objectives
Survive 10 minutes on each planet
Defeat the boss at minute 10
Collect meteorite pieces
Progress through the solar system

...
Players can revisit any planet to:
Complete achievements
Re-learn or review space facts
The Sun unlocks after all 8 planets and features:
A final challenge
Optional educational quiz or science test

More features will be added sooner or later!
